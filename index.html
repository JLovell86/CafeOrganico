About Us.

Our History.

We started with a vision to build a space that fosters connection and community. A space that you can have great conversations with friends or curl up with a good book. We are passionate about sourcing our coffee from small, single origin farms, while providing you with an experience that makes you feel like you are at home. We take time to serve, connect, and build relationships.

About our owners.

During the pandemic, we realized the importance of community and connection. After visiting small family owned coffee farms in Colombia last year, we decided to turn our love for community & our love for coffee into a space that fosters just that. Through Café Organico, we get to connect, laugh, and share while giving back to the community we fell in love with many years ago.

FAQ

Do you ship coffee beans?

Yes! Any of the beans we are featuring in the shop can be shipped. Shipping costs will vary depending on the location.

Do you offer gluten-free pasteries?

Our baked goods are always rotating depending on what our friends at Basta makes that week! Most weeks we do get at least one gluten-free option, but this cannot be guaranteed.
